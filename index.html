About Our Company



At KH Creative, we’re dedicated to delivering high-quality media services with a focus on precision, creativity, and customer satisfaction.
Whether it’s capturing the emotions of a wedding or helping a business stand out, we ensure every detail is thoughtfully executed from start to finish. Led by founder Kalam Hussain, who has worked in the media and film industry for eight years, KH Creative is built on experience, skill, and a passion for storytelling. Kalam was mentored by renowned photographer Paul Noble BIPPA, working alongside him for years before Paul’s well-earned retirement in 2023. To continue Paul’s commitment to excellence, Kalam formed KH Creative, bringing together a team of talented professionals who share his drive for producing outstanding content. We’ve refined our focus to what we do best—Weddings & Events and Brands & Businesses—offering an end-to-end solution for our clients. From concept to final edit, we handle every step with care, ensuring a smooth experience and a polished result. Our attention to detail, high production standards, and efficient turnaround times set us apart. For businesses looking for ongoing support, our Creative Plan Subscription offers flexible content creation tailored to your needs. You pay as you go and enjoy up to 40% off standard filming and editing rates, along with priority booking and express turnaround times. It’s the perfect way to maintain high-quality, consistent content without the hassle. We also now offer graphic design and website development services, helping brands stand out with eye-catching visuals that complement our video work.
